What I enjoy doing..



I love working with data of all types. But the most intriguing one that I found so far is text. Text and language are so intimately tied with humans that we have been developing it for a long time - from hieroglyph to modern age programming languages. Humans have a tendency of accumulating and passing it off to the next generations, whether it may be riches or knowledge.New ways of treasuring knowledge were invented with time, but the one that stayed was writing.There are so many things we can explore with "mere" writings. My interests are closely associated with studying human interations based on their expressions through their writings, hence I enjoy all tasks realted to natural language processing. I also enjoy playing with numbers, as oppose to the writings, they are very less expressive. It is almost like a mystery game when it comes to such numerical type of data.
